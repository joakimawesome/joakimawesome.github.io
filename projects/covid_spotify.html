COVID-19 Lockdowns vs. Music Trends

As a music lover and data enthusiast, I'm eager to explore the relationship between COVID-19 lockdowns and people's musical preferences. With so many of us spending more time at home and seeking ways to cope with the pandemic, I'm curious to see if there have been any significant shifts in what people are listening to on Spotify. By examining the streaming data, I hope to identify trends and patterns that may reveal how lockdowns have impacted our musical tastes. With this information, I believe I can gain a deeper understanding of how people are responding to the pandemic and find ways to enhance our overall well-being through music.

Tools

  • Pandas
  • Matplotlib
  • Spotify API
